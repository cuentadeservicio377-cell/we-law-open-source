# End-To-End Operating Flow

## Scenario: New Client Folder With Transcripts

1. The lawyer gives Hermes a Drive folder or uploads transcripts through the dashboard.
2. Hermes creates a command record and asks the Legal Brain whether this is a known client or matter.
3. Hermes creates or updates the client and matter.
4. Intake extracts facts, missing information and requested scope from transcripts.
5. Records Manager builds the live file and source index.
6. Google Sheets Data Clerk prepares Control Master rows.
7. Legal Research identifies legal basis, risks and required documents.
8. Hermes delegates work to the relevant Paperclip workers.
9. Document, privacy, IP, litigation, deadlines, billing and editorial workers produce role artifacts.
10. Senior Legal Reviewer checks evidence, placeholders, cross-document consistency and readiness.
11. Dashboard shows status, blockers, missing facts, documents, tasks, deadlines and approvals.
12. The lawyer approves, corrects or blocks delivery.

## Done Means

A matter is not done merely because a worker closed an issue. It is done only when artifacts exist, missing information is classified, review gates agree, Workspace state is recorded, dashboard state is visible and the lawyer approval gate is satisfied.
