# WSC.Lat And We Law Research Origins

We Law Open Source comes from WSC.Lat and We Law experimentation around practical legal automation for Spanish-speaking legal practice. The research question is simple but demanding: can a lawyer operate a small legal office through an agentic system without losing professional control, source traceability or client trust?

The answer in this repository is architectural: keep the lawyer accountable, make Hermes coordinate, make Paperclip workers produce auditable work product, keep Workspace familiar, and show everything in a dashboard that feels like a legal office instead of a software control panel.
