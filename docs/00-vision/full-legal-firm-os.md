# Full Legal Firm OS Vision

We Law OS is meant to be a complete legal firm operating system, not an isolated integration. The goal is to let a lawyer run daily legal work with agentic assistance while preserving human control, professional review, client confidentiality and traceability.

## Why A Full OS?

A legal workflow is not just document drafting. A real firm needs client intake, matter opening, source review, folders, memory, task delegation, deadlines, billing blockers, document production, review, approvals, communications and follow-up. If any layer is invisible, the lawyer loses trust.

## The Operating Promise

A lawyer should be able to say: this is the client, this is the folder, these are the transcripts, this is what we agreed to do. Hermes should turn that into a structured matter, Paperclip workers should execute role-specific work, Google Workspace should hold the artifacts, and the dashboard should show what exists, what is missing, what is blocked and what needs approval.

## What Public Contributors Can Build

- Better dashboard surfaces for legal certainty.
- Safer Hermes orchestration and memory rules.
- Better Paperclip worker contracts and validators.
- Workspace adapters that keep Drive, Docs and Sheets human-friendly.
- Legal fixtures that test real operational problems.
- Review gates that prevent fake readiness.
