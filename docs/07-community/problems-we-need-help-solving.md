# Problems We Need Help Solving

We Law OS is intentionally public because the hard problems need community review.

## Legal Memory

How should an AI legal firm remember clients and matters safely without exposing confidential information or corrupting institutional truth?

## Transcript Intake

How should workers extract facts from transcripts while preserving evidence links, corrections, uncertainty and missing data?

## Legal Drafting Quality

How do we prevent drafting agents from shrinking complex legal documents into short summaries? What artifacts should prove that a document used the correct facts and legal basis?

## Package Review

How should a multi-document legal package be reviewed across definitions, parties, dates, amounts, privacy roles, ownership terms, deadlines and signature blocks?

## Workspace Automation

How should Drive, Docs and Sheets stay familiar to lawyers and also readable/writable by agents?

## Dashboard Trust

What should a non-technical lawyer see to trust an agentic office without needing logs or terminals?

## Worker Accountability

How should Paperclip workers behave like staff with roles, tools, blockers and accountability instead of generic agents?

## Proactive Hermes

How can Hermes be proactive with tasks, deadlines, reminders and communications without bypassing lawyer control?

## Privacy

How can an open-source legal-agent system protect client confidentiality by default?
